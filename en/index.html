About Us

Our team is engaged in a wide range of services on the Internet - from website adjustments to the creation of a multilingual portal. Our main task is to make the maximum increase in calls and requests to the customer at its minimum cost.

Before creating the website, we study the business of the customer and develop the design and functionality for specific tasks. We do not use templates, do not create websites on constructors, but create projects on “clean code”. These sites have higher download speeds, they are better perceived by search engines. The main thing is that this approach allows us to create complex functionality - filtering systems, user registration, service catalogs, price calculators. All this makes the site not just a page on the Internet, but an effective tool for attracting customers.

Typically, the development of the site takes time from two weeks to a month, depending on the complexity of the functionality and design. Website development consists of several stages. It begins with the solution of primary problems. At this stage, we stipulate the main points, define the goals and audience of the site. The next important step is the creation of corporate design, which you will be able to identify without any problems.

We develop websites of any complexity and budget and work with different companies.

We work with the most modern tools for developing projects such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES5, ES6), JQuery, Angular, ReactJS, Redux, VueJS, Vuex, SASS, Stylus, Bootstrap, Materialize, WebPack, Gulp.
